Bank Customer Churn Analysis
Executive Summary
1. Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most critical challenges in the banking industry. Retaining existing customers is significantly more cost-effective than acquiring new ones, making churn analysis an important part of business strategy. This project analyzes customer behavior using SQL Server and Power BI to identify patterns associated with customer attrition and provide actionable business insights.

The project combines SQL-based exploratory analysis with an interactive Power BI dashboard, enabling stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify high-risk customer segments, and make informed business decisions.

2. Business Problem

Banks lose customers due to various factors such as demographics, financial behavior, product usage, and customer engagement. Without understanding these patterns, banks may struggle to implement effective retention strategies, leading to revenue loss and reduced customer lifetime value.

The challenge is to identify:

Which customers are most likely to churn.
Which customer segments have the highest churn rates.
Which business factors influence customer retention.
3. Project Objectives

The primary objectives of this project are:

Analyze customer churn using SQL Server.
Build an executive Power BI dashboard for business users.
Compare churn across different customer segments.
Identify high-risk customer groups based on demographic and financial attributes.
Provide data-driven recommendations to improve customer retention.
4. Dataset Overview

The analysis is based on a bank customer dataset containing 10,000 customer records.

The dataset includes customer information such as:

Customer ID
Geography
Gender
Age
Credit Score
Account Balance
Estimated Salary
Number of Products
Tenure
Credit Card Ownership
Active Member Status
Customer Churn Status (Exited)

These attributes allow detailed analysis of customer behavior and churn patterns.

7. Business Value

This dashboard enables decision-makers to:

Monitor customer churn trends.
Identify customer segments with higher churn risk.
Compare churn across countries and customer demographics.
Support data-driven retention strategies.
Improve customer relationship management through actionable insights.
